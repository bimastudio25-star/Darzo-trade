Static Phase 3 labels were not usable. Direction recovery and direction inference governance are complete, with the frozen direction rule `adelin_v2_pre_decision_sweep_v1`.

The bounded GOOD_FAST_REACTION vs FAST_FAILURE diagnostic execution completed at commit `60824ca`. It was signed, pre-registered, and leakage-checked. The verdict was `MIXED_AMBIGUOUS_SMALL_N`, and Phase 4 remains blocked.

Current diagnostic counts:

- GOOD_FAST_REACTION: 10

## Why Expanded/New Samples Are Needed

The current GOOD sample is too small. GOOD_FAST_REACTION N = 10 triggered the minimum-N gate, so even visibly large descriptive differences could not produce a stronger verdict.

There is no clean separation. Confidence-3-only sensitivity amplified some descriptive effects, but the confidence-3 subsample is very small and likely noisy. The next step is not Phase 4. The next step is to pre-register how expanded or new samples would be collected and how the two frozen hypotheses would be checked.

## Frozen Hypotheses

### Post-hoc Hypothesis-Origin Disclosure

H1 and H2 were selected from a prior underpowered exploratory diagnostic with GOOD_FAST_REACTION N=10, FAST_FAILURE N=27, and final verdict `MIXED_AMBIGUOUS_SMALL_N`.

They are post-hoc hypotheses, not validated features. Pre-registering them now does not validate their origin. The expanded/new-sample plan is allowed only to test whether these hypotheses repeat on new or expanded samples. The future test may reject both hypotheses.

These hypotheses must not be treated as edge, filters, scoring components, or deployment evidence.

## Decision Matrix

### A. Hypotheses Repeat With Sufficient N

If H1/H2 repeat directionally on expanded samples and minimum-N gates pass:

- allow bounded confirmatory diagnostic
- still no automatic Phase 4

### B. H1/H2 Fail To Repeat

If candidate feature directions do not repeat:

- reject as sample-specific
- pause Adelin v2 strategy path
- keep as research lab only

### C. Only Confidence-2 Signal

If signal exists only in confidence-2 direction-inferred samples:

- mark weak
- no Phase 4
- require more confidence-3 samples

### D. Minimum-N Not Met

If minimum-N gates fail:

- verdict capped at `MIXED_AMBIGUOUS_SMALL_N`
- more samples required
- no Phase 4

### E. Leakage Detected

If a feature uses post-entry information:

- reject
- no Phase 4
